Rules
Updated 12/29/14

While our network may be a great place to hang around, and play in, we also have the capability of being targeted by meanies :(, and individuals endowed by ignorance. To prevent those trolls from taking your candy, we have a team of dedicated staff members that enforce the rules that we have established :D, but they aren't on 24/7 meaning that sometimes our staff cannot handle a dispute right then and there, and we ask you to respect such rules, and report those that don't. Such rules enlisted in the 'General Rules section apply not only to our in-game community but to our forum community as well. The rules listed should just be common sense, and pretty much describes what 90% of all minecraft servers/networks ask you not to do.

General Rules

  1. Maintain a peaceful and clean environment through chat, excessive swearing and/or spam is NOT permitted.
  2. Avoid using all CAPS.
  3. Use proper grammar while using the chat function.
  4. Do not provide links to Objectionable Material, this results in a permanent ban and CANNOT be appealed for. We don't encourage it, nor do we allow it.
  5. Do not impersonate another member of the community
  6. Follow instructions that a staff member gives, they are here to help, not destroy.
  7. If have found a staff member doing something wrong, or at least you know for sure is wrong, report the issue privately to an Administrator due to the situation involving a staff member.
  8. Racism is not tolerated, and we won't be hesitant to kick you out.
  9. Do not advertise, unless you're trying to direct a specific player, you may use the /msg function.
    10.

*Violation of any rules listed above, may result in consequences depending on it's severity.

In-Game Rules

  1. Do not use hacks of any type (Kill aura, Autolcicker, X-ray, Better Sprinting, Aimbot, etc.) they're endowed to make the game less fun :'(.
  2. Do not raid(grief) your team's supply/resources, they are there for not just yourself, but your team-mates.
  3. Do not encourage other players to violate our rules.
  4. Use an appropriate skin, you'll be kicked for it.
  5. Inappropriate IGN's are not allowed, we'll ask you to change it by switching accounts, or give a temporary nickname if it holds up. Not necessarily ban-worthy.

Forum Rules

  1. Do not necro-post, please look at the dates of the thread/posts before replying.
  2. Do not engage in conversations that involve spam, the thread will eventually be locked.
  3. Do not discuss bans, as it can start a flame war.
  4. Do not make threads that insult other users. We want to keep the community as clean as possible.
  5. If you've made a thread/post that involves criticism, we allow that with the exception of it having to be delivered with politeness.
  6. Under certain exceptions, you are allowed to make threads to whereas you're going to leave/quit the server, but to avoid dramatic responses, the staff will lock it soon after. The forums are to discuss the game mechanics, and such.

*If you feel that a thread and/or post(s) are not following our forum rules, do not be hesitant to report it. False reports will result with an infraction. The staff tend to do what they feel is right, and in the end, they are.

Client Modifications

We do allow a few Client Modifications which allow you to enhance your client, but nothing over the top. Be aware that any client modifcations that give you a higher advantage while playing against vanilla users is not allowed, and will be handled promptly. Below is a list of what you can use.

  1. ArmorStatusHUD/StatusEffectHUD
  2. Optifine
  3. Texture Packs
  4. Toggle-Crouch
